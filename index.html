About Us

A warm corner
in the city of Tirana

Cep Drink is your neighbourhood café-bar tucked on Rruga Tish Dahia, Tirana. Whether you're grabbing your morning espresso, meeting a friend for drinks, or just needing a quiet spot to unwind — we're here for it.

We believe great moments start with great service. Our team is always ready to welcome you with a smile, a fresh brew, and a relaxed atmosphere that feels like home.
